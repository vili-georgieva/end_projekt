Help Page for the Hotel Booking Website

Welcome to our hotel booking website! Here you will find a brief user guide and frequently asked questions (FAQs) to help you navigate our platform.

User Guide

1. Registration and Login

  • Registration: To make a booking, you first need to register. Click on the "Register" button and fill out the form with your personal information.
  • Login: After registration, you can log in using your email address and password.

2. Hotel Search

  • Search Field: Enter the desired location, check-in and check-out dates, and the number of guests in the search field.
  • Filter Options: Use the filters to narrow down your search by price, star rating, amenities, and more.

3. Select a Hotel

  • Hotel Overview: Click on a hotel to see more information, images, and reviews.
  • Check Availability: Verify the availability for your desired dates.

4. Booking

  • Booking Form: Fill out the booking form with your personal details.
  • Payment Methods: Choose your preferred payment method and enter the required payment information.
  • Confirmation: After completing the booking, you will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

5. Managing Your Bookings

  • View Bookings: Log in to your account to view your current and past bookings.
  • Cancellations: Information about cancellations can be found in the confirmation email or in the "My Bookings" section on the website.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How can I reset my password?

If you have forgotten your password, click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the instructions to create a new password.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. The available options will be displayed during the booking process.

3. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking. The cancellation policy can be found in the confirmation email or in the "My Bookings" section on the website.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM.

How can I modify my reservation?

You can modify your reservation by logging into your account or by contacting our support team.

Is breakfast included in my stay?

Breakfast is included in all room rates unless specified otherwise. Please check your booking details for confirmation.

Booking Assistance

If you need help with your booking, please follow these steps:

  • Visit our booking page.
  • Enter your travel dates and room preferences.
  • If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team for assistance.
